# Cardioton Ingredients - Complete Ayurvedic Formula

Cardioton combines six powerful Ayurvedic herbs, each carefully selected for their proven cardiovascular benefits. This natural formulation has been used for centuries in traditional Indian medicine to support heart health and maintain healthy blood pressure levels.

---

## Arjuna Bark (Terminalia arjuna)

**Primary Benefits:**
- Cardiac tonic that strengthens heart muscle
- Supports healthy cholesterol levels
- Improves coronary artery blood flow
- Contains powerful antioxidants

**Traditional Use:**
Arjuna has been the cornerstone of Ayurvedic cardiac care for over 2,500 years. Ancient texts describe it as a "cardiac tonic" that supports overall heart function. Modern research has validated these traditional uses, showing Arjuna's ability to support cardiac muscle strength and improve cardiovascular efficiency.

**Active Compounds:**
- Triterpenoids (arjunolic acid)
- Flavonoids (quercetin, kaempferol)
- Glycosides (arjunetin)
- Tannins and minerals

**Research Insights:**
Studies have shown that Arjuna bark extract may help reduce angina episodes and improve exercise tolerance in patients with cardiovascular concerns. Its antioxidant properties help protect heart tissue from oxidative damage.

---

## Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

**Primary Benefits:**
- Powerful adaptogen for stress management
- Supports healthy blood pressure
- Reduces cortisol levels
- Enhances overall vitality

**Traditional Use:**
Known as "Indian Ginseng," Ashwagandha is one of Ayurveda's most revered herbs. It has been used for millennia to promote vitality, longevity, and stress resilience. In Cardioton, it plays a crucial role in managing the stress-related factors that contribute to hypertension.

**Active Compounds:**
- Withanolides (withaferin A)
- Alkaloids
- Saponins
- Choline

**Research Insights:**
Clinical studies have demonstrated Ashwagandha's ability to significantly reduce cortisol levels. High cortisol is strongly associated with elevated blood pressure, making this herb invaluable for comprehensive cardiovascular support.

---

## Garlic Extract (Allium sativum)

**Primary Benefits:**
- Natural vasodilation support
- Helps maintain healthy cholesterol
- Supports healthy blood circulation
- Contains sulfur compounds for cardiovascular health

**Traditional Use:**
Garlic has been used across virtually all traditional medicine systems for cardiovascular support. Ancient Egyptian, Greek, Chinese, and Indian medical texts all document garlic's heart-healthy properties. Modern science has identified the sulfur compounds responsible for these benefits.

**Active Compounds:**
- Allicin
- Ajoene
- Diallyl disulfide
- S-allyl cysteine

**Research Insights:**
Multiple studies confirm garlic's ability to support healthy blood pressure levels. The sulfur compounds help promote vasodilation, allowing blood vessels to relax and maintain optimal blood flow. Garlic also supports healthy lipid profiles.

---

## Triphala

**Primary Benefits:**
- Powerful antioxidant properties
- Supports healthy digestion
- Promotes detoxification
- Gentle cleansing effect

**Traditional Use:**
Triphala, meaning "three fruits," is a cornerstone Ayurvedic formula composed of Amalaki, Bibhitaki, and Haritaki. This combination has been used for over 2,000 years as a gentle detoxifier and rejuvenative. In Cardioton, it supports the body's natural detoxification processes.

**Composition:**
- **Amalaki** (Emblica officinalis): Rich in vitamin C, supports immunity
- **Bibhitaki** (Terminalia bellirica): Supports respiratory and digestive health
- **Haritaki** (Terminalia chebula): Known as the "King of Medicine" in Ayurveda

**Research Insights:**
Studies show Triphala's exceptional antioxidant capacity, helping to neutralize free radicals that can damage blood vessels. Its gentle cleansing action supports overall metabolic health, which is essential for cardiovascular wellness.

---

## Sarpagandha (Rauwolfia serpentina)

**Primary Benefits:**
- Traditional herb for BP management
- Calming effect on nervous system
- Supports healthy blood pressure levels
- Natural tranquility support

**Traditional Use:**
Sarpagandha has been used in Ayurveda for centuries to support cardiovascular calm and nervous system balance. Its name means "snake-smelling" in Sanskrit, referring to the root's appearance. Modern medicine isolated reserpine from this plant, validating its traditional uses.

**Active Compounds:**
- Reserpine
- Ajmaline
- Serpentine
- Rauwolfinine

**Research Insights:**
Sarpagandha is one of the most researched Ayurvedic herbs for cardiovascular support. Its alkaloids work by supporting the parasympathetic nervous system, promoting a state of calm that benefits blood pressure regulation.

---

## Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri)

**Primary Benefits:**
- Supports healthy circulation
- Promotes cognitive function
- Stress relief properties
- Antioxidant support

**Traditional Use:**
Brahmi, named after Brahma the creator, is considered one of Ayurveda's most important brain tonics. However, its benefits extend to cardiovascular health through its stress-reducing and circulation-supporting properties. It has been used for over 3,000 years in Ayurvedic practice.

**Active Compounds:**
- Bacosides A and B
- Brahmine
- Herpestine
- D-mannitol

**Research Insights:**
Research demonstrates Brahmi's adaptogenic properties, helping the body manage stress more effectively. By supporting cognitive function and reducing stress, Brahmi indirectly supports cardiovascular health through the mind-heart connection.
