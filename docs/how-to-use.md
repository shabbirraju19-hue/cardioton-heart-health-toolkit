# How to Use Cardioton - Complete Dosage Guide

This guide covers everything you need to know about using Cardioton effectively, including dosage, timing, what to expect, and tips for maximizing results.

---

## Recommended Dosage

### Standard Dosage

**Adults:** 1-2 capsules twice daily

**Timing:**
- Morning dose: 30 minutes before breakfast
- Evening dose: 30 minutes before dinner

**With Water:** Take with a full glass of room-temperature water

## Best Practices for Maximum Results

### Timing Matters

**Take consistently:** Same times every day helps maintain steady levels of active compounds in your system.

**Before meals:** Taking 30 minutes before meals allows for optimal absorption.

**Avoid late night:** Evening dose should be at least 2 hours before bedtime for best results.

### Lifestyle Support

**Exercise:**
- Light to moderate exercise 30 minutes daily
- Walking, yoga, or swimming are excellent choices
- Avoid strenuous workouts immediately after taking Cardioton

**Diet:**
- Reduce sodium intake (less than 2,300mg daily)
- Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables
- Limit processed and fried foods
- Stay hydrated (8-10 glasses of water daily)

**Stress Management:**
- Practice deep breathing exercises
- Consider meditation or mindfulness
- Ensure adequate sleep (7-8 hours)
- Take breaks during work hours

### What to Avoid

**While Taking Cardioton:**
- Excessive alcohol consumption
- Smoking
- High-sodium processed foods
- Extreme stress without management
- Irregular sleep patterns

## Special Considerations

### For Active Individuals

If you exercise regularly:
- Take Cardioton at least 1 hour before workouts
- Stay well-hydrated
- Monitor how your body responds

### For Shift Workers

If you work irregular hours:
- Take doses relative to your wake time
- Maintain consistent intervals between doses
- Example: Wake time + 1 hour, then 12 hours later

### For Older Adults (60+)

- Start with 1 capsule twice daily
- Gradually increase if well-tolerated
- Monitor BP more frequently
- Consult healthcare provider regularly

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I take Cardioton with other supplements?

Generally yes, but space them 2 hours apart. Avoid taking with other blood pressure supplements without consulting a healthcare provider.

### What if I miss a dose?

Take it when you remember, unless it's close to your next scheduled dose. Don't double up. Resume your regular schedule.

### Can I take Cardioton on an empty stomach?

It's recommended to take 30 minutes before meals. Some users with sensitive stomachs prefer taking with a small amount of food.

### How long should I take Cardioton?

Cardioton can be taken long-term as part of a heart-healthy lifestyle. Many users take it continuously for ongoing cardiovascular support.

### Will I see immediate results?

Natural supplements work gradually. Most users notice initial benefits within 2-4 weeks, with optimal results at 8-12 weeks of consistent use.

### Can I stop taking Cardioton once my BP improves?

Cardioton supports cardiovascular wellness as part of a healthy lifestyle. Discuss any changes to your regimen with your healthcare provider.

## Storage Instructions

- Store in a cool, dry place
- Away from direct sunlight
- Keep bottle tightly closed
- Out of reach of children

## When to Consult a Healthcare Provider

**Seek medical advice if:**
- You have existing medical conditions
- You're taking prescription medications
- You're pregnant or nursing
- You experience any unusual symptoms
- Your BP readings remain elevated after 8 weeks

**Remember:** Cardioton is a dietary supplement, not a replacement for medical care. Always work with your healthcare provider for cardiovascular health management.
